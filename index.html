Background Story
King Shahryar

King Shahryar had a first wife whom he loved the most, however One day he discovered her cheating with someone else which led him to believe that all women would eventually cheat on him.

King Shahryar vowed to marry every maiden in his territory but with a bad intention, the night after the marriage he would behead his wife, killing her even before sunrise.

King Shahryar married and killed hundreds of his wives before he met Scheherazade which after telling him stories for 1,001 nights, changed his heart and made him stop the cycle of marrying and killing his wives.
